Firestick Setup Guide

Step 1: Access Developer Options

  1. Go to Settings on your Firestick.
  2. Scroll right to find My Fire TV and select it.
  3. Choose About and click on your device name multiple times until a
    "You are now a developer" popup appears.

Step 2: Allow Apps from Unknown Sources

  1. From Settings, select Device / My Fire TV.
  2. Navigate to Developer options.
  3. Locate Apps from Unknown Sources and toggle it on.
  4. Confirm your selection by clicking Turn On.

Step 3: Download STB Emu App

Before moving forward, please make sure you have installed the Downloader app (Orange Logo).

  1. On your FireStick home screen, click My Apps.
  2. Navigate to the Downloader app and open it.
  3. Click the URL box.
  4. Enter the code 9386189 or https://bit.ly/websmpro and click Go, then wait for the APK file to download.
  5. Click Install.
  6. Click Done.
  7. Click Delete to remove the APK file from your FireStick.
  8. Click Delete again to confirm.

Next Step

Once these steps are completed, contact your provider to receive the next steps.
